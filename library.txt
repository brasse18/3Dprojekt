Differences between floating point grammar in cpp and hlsl. 
(float = 32bit, double = 64bit)
.cpp:
	1.0f, 1.0F = float
	1.0  = double
	1.0l, 1.0L = long double
.hlsl: 
	1.0  = float
	1.0L = double
